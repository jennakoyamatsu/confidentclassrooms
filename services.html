Services
Does public speaking make you nervous? It tends to make everyone nervous. Imagine if we caught students before bad habits developed, before they learned to hate it. With Confident Classrooms students will learn behaviors that will last a lifetime. They will begin to see speaking as an opportunity and not a threat. According to ELA State Standards for Speaking and Listening, all students are expected to become effective communicators*. However, students often don't have the strategies to be successful. This is where Confident Classrooms can help your students find success. www.ccss.ela-literacy

Confident Classrooms offers workshops targeted for teacher and staff development, as well as direct classroom instruction for students at every grade level. Whole class and small group instruction (ideal for English Language Learners) and parent workshops are available.

TEACHER WORKSHOPS

Teacher workshops train the full staff at a meeting. The workshop is experiential and meant for teachers to brainstorm and experience what the kids will learn. At the end of each workshop teachers will walk away with actions to take in their classroom to increase the success of students' public speaking and communication skills. (60 min)

STUDENT WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended to have Amy work with the whole class on a given communication topic or strategy. The students will actively participate and have strategies for their next communication event.
(30 min)

STUDENT SMALL
GROUP WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended for a target group of students. An easy time for this is in centers or when other students are already engaged in other projects. Often this time is spent with English Language Learners as an opportunity to improve their skills
(30 min)
