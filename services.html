Services
Confident Classrooms offers workshops targeted for teacher and staff development, as well as direct classroom instruction for students at every grade level. Whole class and small group instruction (ideal for English Language Learners)
and parent workshops are available.

TEACHER WORKSHOPS

Training for the full staff/faculty, typically at a staff meeting. The workshop is experiential and meant for teachers to brainstorm and experience what the kids will learn. At the end of each workshop teachers will walk away with actions to take in their classroom to increase the success of students' public speaking and communication skills. (60 min)

STUDENT WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended to have Amy work with the whole class on a given communication topic or strategy. The students will actively participate and have strategies for their next communication event.
(30 min)

STUDENT SMALL
GROUP WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended for a target group of students. An easy time for this is in centers or when other students are already engaged in other projects. Often this time is spent with English Language Learners as an opportunity to improve their skills
(30 min)
