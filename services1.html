Services
Does public speaking make you nervous? It tends to make everyone nervous. Imagine if we caught students before bad habits developed, before they learned to fear it. With Confident Classrooms students will learn behaviors that will last a lifetime. They will begin to see speaking as an opportunity and not a threat.

According to ELA State Standards for Speaking and Listening, all students are expected to become effective communicators. These standards are "designed to prepare students for life outside the classroom"*. However, students often don't have the strategies to be successful. This is where Confident Classrooms can help your students find success. Teachers will value the concrete approach that they can integrate into their already existing curriculum.
*See ELA Standards

Confident Classrooms offers workshops targeted for teacher and staff development, as well as direct classroom instruction for students at every grade level. Whole class and small group instruction (ideal for English Language Learners)
and parent workshops are available.

TEACHER WORKSHOPS

Teacher workshops train the full staff at a meeting. The workshop is experiential and meant for teachers to brainstorm and experience what the kids will learn. At the end of each workshop teachers will walk away with actions to take in their classroom to increase the success of students' public speaking and communication skills. (60 min)

STUDENT WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended to have Amy work with the whole class on a given communication topic or strategy. The students will actively participate and have strategies for the next time they want to speak up in class, participate in a class discussion, give a presentation, or even answer a question.
(30 min)

STUDENT SMALL
GROUP WORKSHOPS

This workshop is intended for a target group of students. An easy time for this is in centers or when other students are already engaged in other projects. Often this time is spent with English Language Learners as an opportunity to improve their skills
(30 min)

PARENT WORKSHOPS

These workshops are meant to be a compliment to the workshops happening in the classrooms with students.
